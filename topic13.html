Topic: Designing, Defining, and Triggering CSS3 Animations without Custom Libraries

Animations

Animations bring a webpage to life. They give the appearance of elements moving on the page. In CSS, there are two main ways to move things on a webpage. The transition property is one way and the other is the animation property + keyframes. On my topic 9 page, it is explaned in more detail. This is more of a collection of animations on this page.

One note on performance and animation: The most Performant Properties for Animation are position, scale, rotation and opacity. These properties do not cause a repaint of elements or a reflow, which is costly from a performance standpoint.
